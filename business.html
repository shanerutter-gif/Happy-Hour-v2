Questions

FAQ

My business isn't listed yet — can I add it?
Yes! In the owner portal you can submit your business for addition. We'll review and add it within 24–48 hours, then hand you the keys.
How do you verify ownership?
We do a manual check — usually a quick look at your website, Google Business listing, or a brief email exchange. It's not automated (yet) so it typically takes less than a day.
Can I list multiple locations?
Yes. Each location gets its own listing. Submit a claim for each one and they'll all be linked to your account.
What if the info on my listing is wrong?
Claim the listing and fix it yourself — that's literally what this is for. Once you're verified you can update anything instantly.
Is this actually free forever?
Yes. The base listing, all editing features, and event posting are free. We may offer optional paid features (like featured placement) in the future — but the core will always be free.
