Best Happy Hours in Orange County (2026)

From waterfront pints in Newport Beach to rooftop cocktails in Laguna Beach — every verified happy hour worth your time in OC, updated for 2026.

By Shane May 29, 2026 10 min read
Share

Orange County is not a cheap place to live, but its happy hours punch well above their weight. You can watch the sunset over Newport Harbor with a $4 beer in hand, sip through South African–inspired cocktails overlooking Laguna's coastline, or find yourself at a rooftop above the Huntington Beach pier before 6pm — all for the price of a mediocre dinner elsewhere. You just have to know where to go and when.

This is the best happy hours guide for Orange County in 2026 — organized by area, covering the venues worth your time, with verified hours and deals for each. Every spot below is confirmed open as of this writing. Spotd tracks current OC happy hours so you always have the latest.

Newport Beach Happy Hours

Newport Beach has the highest concentration of reliable happy hour spots in OC — and a few that are genuinely exceptional. The competition keeps standards up.

Woody's Wharf

Happy hour weekdays 3–6pm, plus Sunday late-night in the bar — $5 well drinks, $4 draft beer, $6 house wine, plus half-price happy hour food including ahi poke tower and grilled fish tacos. Woody's Wharf sits right on the Newport Harbor waterfront at 2318 Newport Blvd, with a classic Southern California dock-bar vibe that's been around for decades. The Sunday late-night bar special is an underrated move if you want to extend the weekend.

Bosscat Kitchen & Libations

Happy hour weekdays 3–6pm — Half-off whiskey pours, $5 beer, $7 wine. If you're a whiskey person, Bosscat Kitchen & Libations is the best deal in OC. They stock over 300 bottles — rare Japanese expressions, Pappy Van Winkle, serious craft American whiskeys — and half-off during happy hour makes this absurdly good value. Food is American comfort with a craft-bar sensibility. At 4647 MacArthur Blvd, it skews toward a professional after-work crowd.

The Pour House

Happy hour daily — $5 draft beer, $6 wine, $7 cocktails. Dependable and unpretentious — the kind of Newport Beach bar that doesn't need a view to justify its existence. Solid deal structure across the board.

Balboa Bar

Happy hour daily — $4 draft beer, $5 wells, $5 wine. One of Newport's most storied dive bars, on the Balboa Peninsula. No pretense, no frills, just good cheap drinks in a spot that's been part of the local fabric for generations. The $4 draft is one of the best prices anywhere in OC.

Muldoon's Irish Pub

Happy hour daily — $4 draft beer, $5 wells, $5 wine. Muldoon's Irish Pub in Newport has been holding happy hour steady for years. A proper pub in the British Isles sense — worth it if you want somewhere that feels distinctly un-Californian.

The Winery Restaurant

Happy hour in the bar area — Half-off wine flights, $9 cocktails, $6 beer. This is the upscale option on this list. The Winery specializes in wine-pairing cuisine and the half-off wine flights during happy hour are genuinely excellent value for what you get. Best for a date or an occasion where the atmosphere matters.

Laguna Beach Happy Hours

Laguna Beach happy hours tend to reward patience — the drinks are priced for the views, and the views are worth it. The key is hitting the patio before the tourist rush.

Mozambique

Happy hour daily (closed Mon–Tue) — $7 cocktails, $6 wine, $5 beer. Mozambique at 1740 S. Coast Hwy is one of the most distinctive bars in all of Southern California. South African influenced, open-air rooftop terrace, live music at their Ivan'z House venue upstairs. The peri-peri prawn appetizers during happy hour are what $5 beer was made to accompany. Don't miss the fire pit area on cooler evenings. Note: closed Mondays and Tuesdays.

Laguna Beach Beer Company

Open daily — happy hour pricing from noon — $4 pints, $5 craft drafts, $6 flights. At 859 Laguna Canyon Rd, this is the local craft beer anchor for Laguna. They recently brought in a new head brewer and launched six new beers — the lineup is strong right now. The flights are the move: six 5oz pours for $6 covers more ground than any single pint.

Oto Sushi

Happy hour daily — Cocktail and sake specials with discounted small plates. Laguna Beach's reliable Japanese option with a patio that fills up fast. The happy hour sake selections cycle regularly.

Huntington Beach Happy Hours

Surf City is less polished than Newport or Laguna — and that's exactly why it's great for happy hour. The energy is casual, the prices are honest, and the pier backdrop is free.

Hurricanes Bar & Grill

Happy hour daily — $4 draft beer, $5 well drinks, $5 house wine. Hurricanes at 200 Main St has been the go-to spot above the pier for over 30 years. It earns that reputation. The rooftop gives you one of the best views of the Huntington Beach pier and the Pacific — and the happy hour prices are genuinely reasonable for a location this good. Arrive before 5pm on weekends or you're waiting for a table.

Misty's

Happy hour daily — Live music nights weekly. Huntington Beach's live music venue with a proper bar setup. The combination of happy hour pricing and regular live acts makes this the best bang-for-buck evening in HB if you want something happening around you.

Inland OC: Fullerton, Santa Ana & Tustin

The inland spots get slept on. Less coastal, less competition, and in some cases the best drink prices in the county.

Matador Cantina — Fullerton

Happy hour daily — $5 margaritas, $3 beer, half-off tacos. This is the best taco-plus-happy-hour combination in OC, and it's not close. Half-off tacos alongside $3 beer is a legitimate steal. Fullerton's bar district around the downtown grid is worth the detour.

Continental Room — Fullerton

Happy hour daily — $7 cocktails, $5 beer, $6 wine. Downtown Fullerton's classic cocktail lounge. Lower-key atmosphere than the college bars nearby — this is where you go when you want a proper drink and a conversation rather than a crowd.

Native Son Alehouse — Santa Ana

Happy hour daily — $1–2 off all drafts, $5 well drinks. Santa Ana's craft beer anchor with a rotating tap list. The dollar-off-drafts deal sounds modest until you realize they're rotating 20+ taps — it adds up quickly and the selection is consistently excellent.

Tustin Brewing Company — Tustin

Happy hour daily — $4 pints, $5 specialty drafts, $6 flights. Brewed on-site. Tustin Brewing is the kind of neighborhood brewery that knows its regulars by name. The specialty draft pricing during happy hour lets you work through the seasonal rotation without commitment — order the flight and see what's new.

Lola Gaspar — Santa Ana

Happy hour daily — Cocktail specials and small plates. In the heart of Santa Ana's Arts District, Lola Gaspar is the city's best cocktail bar with a Latin-influenced menu. The space is genuinely beautiful — exposed brick, low lighting — and the bartenders know what they're doing with spirits.

Tips for OC Happy Hour

Go early, especially in summer. Waterfront spots in Newport and Laguna fill up fast on warm evenings. Arriving at 3:30pm rather than 5:30pm is a different experience entirely — you get a table, better service, and the pre-sunset light is actually nicer anyway.

Inland OC is underrated. Fullerton's downtown bar district and Santa Ana's Arts District have some of the best drink prices in the county and a genuinely local crowd. If you're coming from the 5 or 91, it's worth skipping the beach traffic entirely and heading to Matador or Native Son instead.

Whiskey drinkers: go to Bosscat first. Half-off rare whiskey is a genuinely exceptional deal that doesn't exist at this quality level many other places in SoCal.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is happy hour in Orange County?

Most Orange County happy hours run weekdays 3–6pm. Newport Beach spots like Woody's Wharf and Bosscat Kitchen run 3–6pm on weekdays. Laguna Beach Beer Company starts from noon daily. Some inland OC spots run daily happy hours.

Where can I get the best whiskey deals in Orange County?

Bosscat Kitchen & Libations at 4647 MacArthur Blvd in Newport Beach is the best whiskey deal in OC — 300+ bottles including rare Japanese expressions, with half-off pours during weekday happy hour 3–6pm.

What are the best waterfront happy hours in Orange County?

Woody's Wharf in Newport Beach (2318 Newport Blvd) is the top waterfront happy hour — $4 draft beer and $5 wells right on Newport Harbor. Mozambique in Laguna Beach offers a rooftop terrace overlooking the Pacific with $5 beer.

Are there good happy hours in inland Orange County?

Yes — inland OC is underrated. Matador Cantina in Fullerton has $3 beers and half-off tacos daily. Native Son Alehouse in Santa Ana runs $1–2 off all 20+ rotating drafts. Both areas are far less crowded than the beach cities.
