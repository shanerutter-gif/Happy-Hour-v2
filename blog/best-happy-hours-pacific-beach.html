Best Happy Hours in Pacific Beach, San Diego (2026)

From $2.50 beers at Rocky's to rooftop sushi at Cannonball — every PB happy hour worth your time, tested and updated for 2026.

By Shane March 31, 2026 9 min read

Pacific Beach has more happy hours per square mile than basically anywhere in San Diego. The problem isn't finding one — it's finding the right one. The one where $3 actually gets you a real drink, not a thimble of warm Bud Light.

We hit every bar on Garnet, Mission, and the boardwalk to put this list together. 48 venues. Every deal verified. Here's the breakdown by vibe so you can skip the research and get straight to the good stuff.

What Makes PB Special for Happy Hour

Most neighborhoods in San Diego have a happy hour scene. PB has a happy hour culture. The entire neighborhood is built around people who want to grab a cheap drink after surfing, biking the boardwalk, or just existing near the ocean.

What sets PB apart:

  • It starts early. Some spots kick off at 9am (looking at you, Coaster Saloon). This isn't a "5pm on a Tuesday" neighborhood.
  • It runs daily. Most PB happy hours go 7 days a week. Weekend happy hours are the norm here, not the exception.
  • The range is wild. You can do $2.50 beers at a legendary dive or $14 chili mango margaritas at an oceanfront hotel. Same neighborhood, completely different experience.
  • Beach proximity. Half these bars are within stumbling distance of the sand. Try getting that in North Park.

Best for Dates

Taking someone out in PB and need it to feel like you put thought into it? These spots deliver ambiance without the Gaslamp price tag.

JRDN Restaurant

Happy hour Mon–Fri 3–5pm — $7 draft beer, $9 house wine, $10 martini, $14 chili mango margarita. The move: their $25 "Low Effort Luxe" combo gets you oysters, caesar, truffle fries, and prosecco. That's a full date for the price of two drinks downtown. Oceanfront at Tower23 Hotel, so the views do the heavy lifting.

Cannonball Restaurant & Bar

Happy hour Tue–Fri 4–6pm — Rooftop sushi specials with ocean views from every table. This is PB's most underrated date spot. The sunset from up here is genuinely incredible and the sushi happy hour prices make it approachable.

The Grass Skirt

Open nightly from 5pm — $30 domestic beer buckets, $15 champagne bottles, $5 mimosas. The tiki bar hidden behind Good Time Poke. Polynesian decor, creative cocktails, and a vibe that feels like you're somewhere way more exotic than Garnet Ave. $15 champagne bottles are a steal for a date night opener.

Flamingo Deck PB

Happy hour daily 3–6pm — $5 draft beer, $8 wine & cocktails, $15 artisan pizzas. Rooftop bar with wood-fired pizza and sunset views over Mission Beach. The $15 truffle mushroom pizza during happy hour is chef's kiss.

Best for Groups

Rolling deep? These spots can handle your whole crew without anyone going broke.

Duck Dive

Happy hour Mon–Fri 3–6pm — $3.50 domestic drafts, $5 craft cocktails, $6 craft drafts, $6.50 duck fat fries, $8.50 chicken tenders. One of PB's best all-around happy hours. The food deals are legit (not just sad nachos), the space is big enough for groups, and the surf-bar vibe keeps everyone happy. Plus trivia on Thursdays at 8pm if your crew is competitive.

Pacific Beach AleHouse

Happy hour Mon–Fri 2–5pm — $5 draft beers & well cocktails, half-price bottles of wine, $10 flatbreads, $15 cheeseburger + draft beer combo. Two stories, a rooftop skydeck with ocean views, and trivia on Wednesdays at 7pm. The $15 burger-and-beer combo is basically why this place exists.

Dirty Birds Pacific Beach

Happy hour 11am–6pm — Half off select pitchers, BOGO half off traditional wings. Six hours of happy hour and the wing deal is hard to beat for a big group. TVs everywhere if there's a game on.

Baja Beach Cafe

Happy hour 4–7pm daily (including weekends) — 2 for 1 drinks, seven days a week. Simple, effective, and right on the boardwalk. When the group chat says "just pick somewhere," this is the move.

Cheapest Drinks

If your main criteria is "spend as little as possible while still having a good time," these are your spots.

Rocky's Crown Pub

Happy hour daily 11am–6pm — $3 wells, domestic beers from $2.50. Cash only, no frills, and consistently one of the best-kept secrets in PB. Rocky's has been doing this for decades and nothing has changed. That's the whole point. $2.50 beers in 2026 is genuinely remarkable.

The Coaster Saloon

Happy hour daily 9am–2pm — $3 beers, $4 wells. Yes, happy hour starts at 9am. This is a beachside dive bar that knows exactly what it is. Stiff pours, no pretense, open every day. If you're up early after a surf session, this is the spot.

The Beachcomber PB

Happy hour daily 11am–5pm — $4 domestics, $5 wells. Six hours of cheap drinks with a beachfront patio and live music on weekends. Probably the best value-to-vibes ratio in PB.

West End

Happy hour daily 11am–6pm — $2 off beer, $7 wells, $8 house wine, $10 cocktails. Plus $8 fries, $12 appetizers, $18 ahi tuna poke. A North PB staple since the 70s with pool tables and a real local crowd. The food deals during happy hour are unusually good for a dive bar.

Best Patio & Oceanview

You're in Pacific Beach. You should be able to see the ocean while you drink. These spots deliver.

Waterbar

Happy hour daily — $2 off all draft beers, $7 bloody marys, $6 sangria. Weekend specials include $6 mimosas and $23 champagne bottles. Literally on the sand. This is as close to the ocean as you can get with a drink in your hand. The weekend champagne bottle deal is a PB brunch staple.

World Famous

Happy hour daily 4–7pm — Half-price oysters, $2 off craft cocktails, $2 off house drafts, $2 off select appetizers. Right on Pacific Beach Drive with views of the boardwalk and ocean. The half-price oysters alone are worth the trip.

Lahaina Beach House

Happy hour specials daily — Cash-only beachfront bar with a massive patio and sunset views. Burgers, tacos, and a vibe that hasn't changed in years. One of the most beloved patios in all of PB.

PB Shore Club

Happy hour specials daily — Outdoor patio with ocean views, bar games, and DJ nights. The upstairs bungalow setup gives it a treehouse feel right near the beach.
