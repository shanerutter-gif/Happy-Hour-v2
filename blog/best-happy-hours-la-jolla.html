Best Happy Hours in La Jolla, San Diego (2026)

George's at the Cove for the view. Roppongi for 50% off tapas. Puesto for the margaritas. These are La Jolla's best happy hour deals — verified for 2026.

D
By Diego June 17, 2026 · 8 min read

La Jolla has a reputation as San Diego's expensive zip code — and honestly, it's earned it. But the happy hour scene here quietly punches above its weight. You've got cliff-side cocktails at George's at the Cove, beachfront Aloha Hour at Duke's, 50% off Asian tapas at Roppongi, and Puesto serving what many locals call the best margarita in the county at half price before 5pm.

This is a neighborhood built for the golden hour. The light hits the Pacific differently here, the cliffs are dramatic, and if you time it right you'll be nursing a craft cocktail while the sun drops into the ocean. These six spots make that easy — and affordable.

1. George's at the Cove — Best View in La Jolla

Happy hour: Daily, 3–7pm
Deals: $8 craft cocktails, $6 draft beers, discounted wine by the glass
Location: 1250 Prospect St (Level2 Ocean Terrace)

If you're only going to one La Jolla happy hour, make it George's at the Cove. The view from the ocean terrace is genuinely one of the best in San Diego — glittering water, sea kayakers in the cove below, and a horizon that seems to go on forever. Happy hour runs daily from 3pm to 7pm with $8 craft cocktails and $6 draft beers, which is remarkable given the address. The mai tai is the move here. Get there before 5pm on weekends if you want a terrace table without a wait.

2. Duke's La Jolla — Aloha Hour on the Water

Happy hour: Daily, 3–6pm (Aloha Hour)
Deals: $8 cocktails, $6 draft beers, appetizer specials
Location: 1216 Prospect St

Two blocks from George's, Duke's La Jolla runs its "Aloha Hour" daily 3–6pm. Hawaiian-inspired cocktails at $8, cold draft beers at $6, and food specials that make it a proper meal destination rather than just a drink stop. The surf-and-aloha vibe is unpretentious compared to some of Prospect Street's stuffier neighbours, and the upstairs bar has a deck overlooking the ocean. Order the surf nachos or the calamari strips and settle in.

3. Puesto La Jolla — Best Margaritas at the Best Price

Happy hour: Monday–Friday, 3–5pm
Deals: Half-off bar snacks, $9 house margaritas, $7 micheladas
Location: 1044 Wall St

Puesto La Jolla has built a devoted following for its crispy tacos and agave program — the Monday–Friday 3–5pm happy hour is where to access all of it at roughly half price. House margaritas drop to $9, micheladas to $7, and the bar snack menu (crispy chicken taquitos, esquite, guac with housemade chips) is half off. The only catch is the 2-hour window on weekdays, so this one rewards the early finish. Regulars know to grab a spot at the bar at exactly 3pm.

4. Roppongi — 50% Off Asian Tapas

Happy hour: Daily, 3–5:30pm
Deals: 50% off Asian tapas menu, $2 off all drinks
Location: 875 Prospect St

This is arguably the best food deal on this list. Roppongi is a La Jolla institution with an Asian fusion tapas menu that would normally be a real splurge. At 3–5:30pm daily the entire tapas menu is 50% off — think ahi tuna poke, Kobe beef skewers, and dynamite shrimp at half their dinner price — plus $2 off everything at the bar. The patio is gorgeous and the vibe is reliably sophisticated without being stiff. Come hungry.

5. We Olive & Wine Bar — Wine Wednesday and Weekday Deals

Happy hour: Monday–Friday, 3:30–6:30pm; Wine Wednesday all day
Deals: Discounted wine pours, olive oil tastings, seasonal small plates
Location: 7853 Girard Ave

We Olive & Wine Bar is the neighbourhood's most underrated find. Part gourmet olive oil shop, part wine bar, the Monday–Friday happy hour (3:30–6:30pm) features rotating wine specials by the glass and small plates. The real headline is Wine Wednesday, where 50% off all pours runs the entire day — making it one of the best wine deals in La Jolla by a wide margin. The small Girard Ave shop fills up fast on Wednesdays; the regulars know the drill.

6. Catania — Italian Rooftop on Girard

Happy hour: Daily, 3–6pm (opens 4pm Wednesdays)
Deals: Discounted cocktails, wine specials, bar bites
Location: 939 Prospect St

Catania brings Italian coastal restaurant energy to one of La Jolla's most scenic rooftop perches. The happy hour bar menu features discounted cocktails and wines, plus Italian-inspired bar bites — think arancini, burrata, and charcuterie boards at approachable prices. The rooftop terrace on Prospect is one of the most photogenic spots in San Diego. Note the slightly later Wednesday opening at 4pm if you're planning a midweek visit.

Tips for La Jolla Happy Hour

La Jolla parking is notoriously tight in the summer — budget an extra 15–20 minutes and use the Prospect Street parking structure on Herschel Ave rather than circling. The walk from there to any of these spots is under five minutes.

The window between 3pm and 5pm hits a sweet spot before the after-work crowd arrives. If you want the ocean terrace at George's or the patio at Roppongi, that's your window. On weekends, George's gets busy from the moment happy hour starts.

The Roppongi 50% off tapas deal is exceptional — order a few rounds of small plates and you'll leave genuinely stuffed for what a starter would cost at dinner. It's the kind of deal that makes La Jolla a legitimate budget destination rather than just a beautiful place to walk past.

For the full La Jolla experience: start at We Olive on Girard, walk down to Roppongi for tapas, and finish at George's at the Cove in time for the sunset. You've just run the La Jolla happy hour trifecta for under $40.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is happy hour in La Jolla?
Most La Jolla happy hours run from 3pm to 6pm or 7pm on weekdays. George's at the Cove runs 3–7pm daily, Duke's Aloha Hour is 3–6pm daily, Puesto is 3–5pm Monday through Friday, and Roppongi goes 3–5:30pm daily.
Where is the best happy hour in La Jolla?
George's at the Cove is the top pick for ocean views and $8 craft cocktails daily 3–7pm. For the best value, head to Roppongi for 50% off Asian tapas and $2 off all drinks daily 3–5:30pm.
Does La Jolla have good happy hour deals?
Yes — La Jolla has a surprisingly strong happy hour scene considering its upscale reputation. Puesto offers half-off bar snacks and $9 house margaritas Monday–Friday, We Olive does 50% off all wine on Wednesdays, and Roppongi's 50% off Asian tapas is one of the best food deals in San Diego.
What are the best bars in La Jolla for happy hour?
The best La Jolla happy hour bars are George's at the Cove (daily 3–7pm, ocean views), Duke's La Jolla (daily 3–6pm Aloha Hour on the water), Puesto La Jolla (Mon–Fri 3–5pm, best margaritas), Roppongi (daily 3–5:30pm, 50% off tapas), We Olive & Wine Bar (Mon–Fri 3:30–6:30pm), and Catania (daily 3–6pm, Italian rooftop on Girard).
