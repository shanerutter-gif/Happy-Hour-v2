About Spotd

"Where should we go?"
deserves a better answer.

Spotd is the go-out app — every happy hour, trivia night, live music set and deal happening near you tonight, in two taps.

Why we built it

Every great night out starts with the same conversation: "where should we go?" And somehow the answer always involves four apps, a group chat, a review from 2019 and a happy hour that ended an hour before you arrived.

Yelp and Google can tell you where to eat. Nobody could tell you where to be — what's actually on tonight, which patio has the game, where the deals are still running, where your friends ended up. So we built it.

Spotd is made by a small independent team that cares a lot about going out well. No venue pays to rank higher. No ads dressed up as recommendations. Just the real picture of your city tonight.

What makes Spotd different

🍹
Built for tonight
Live deals, happy hour windows and events happening right now — not reviews from last March.
🎯
Filters that get it
"Dog-friendly patio with the game on" is a totally reasonable thing to want. We're the only place you can find it.
📍
A real social layer
Check in, see where friends landed, and watch your city's night unfold in the feed.
🆓
Free. Actually free.
Spotd is free for users, always. Venues can list for free too — we make money on optional pro tools for businesses.
