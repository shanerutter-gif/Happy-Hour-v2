Common questions
My venue is already on Spotd — how do I claim it? ▾
Search for your venue name in the claim flow, select it, and fill in your contact info. We verify claims within 24 hours and then you'll have full edit access to your listing.
What if my venue isn't on Spotd yet? ▾
If we can't find your venue in the search, use the "Submit it for addition" link in the claim flow. We'll add it and notify you — usually within 24 hours.
How often can I update my deals and hours? ▾
As often as you want. Log in to your portal and changes go live immediately — no approval required. We recommend keeping your deals current so customers always see accurate info.
What cities is Spotd available in? ▾
San Diego is fully live with 400+ venues. Los Angeles, New York, Chicago, Austin, Miami, and Orange County are launching soon. Claim your listing now to be ready when your city goes live.
Is there a contract or commitment? ▾
None. Spotd is free forever for venue owners. No credit card, no lock-in, no cancellation fees.
